History

EcoCrafts was founded in 2010 by a group of eco-conscious artisans passionate about creating sustainable and innovative products. Initially, we specialized in handcrafted items made from recycled materials. Over the years, we have grown to become a trusted brand offering a wide range of eco-friendly products.

Why Choose Us?

  • Our commitment to sustainability and eco-friendly practices.
  • Unique, high-quality products crafted with care.
  • Support for local artisans and fair trade initiatives.
